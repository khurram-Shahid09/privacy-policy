Privacy Policy for Birds Lovers

Last updated: January 3, 2025

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application Birds Lovers, developed by Khurram Shahid ("we," "our," or "us"). This document explains how we collect, use, protect, and share your personal and sensitive data.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us at birdlovershelp@gmail.com.

Summary of Key Points

  • What data we collect
  • How we use and share your data
  • How we keep your data safe
  • Data retention and deletion policy
  • Your rights and how to exercise them

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following data to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Data: Names, email addresses, usernames, phone numbers, and profile photos.
  • Messaging Data: Messages sent within the app are stored temporarily for facilitating communication and resolving disputes.
  • Phone Numbers: Users may optionally provide their phone numbers when posting ads. This information is collected solely to facilitate communication between buyers and sellers, based on the user's choice to share this information.
  • Device Data: Information about the device you use, such as IP address, browser type, and operating system.

2. How We Use and Share Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Facilitate user registration and account management.
  • Enable in-app messaging and transaction features.
  • Communicate with you about updates, promotions, and support.
  • Analyze app usage to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Optional Contact Information: Phone numbers provided by users are displayed in ads only when users explicitly choose to allow contact through their phone numbers. This data is not used for authentication or marketing purposes.

We share your data only in these circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Limited information, such as username and transaction details, is shared to facilitate transactions.
  • With Third Parties: Service providers who assist with analytics, and cloud data storage (e.g., Firebase), bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Obligations: If required by law, we may disclose data to comply with legal processes or prevent fraud.

3. How We Keep Your Data Safe

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption for data storage and transmission.
  • Regular monitoring for security vulnerabilities.
  • Access control mechanisms to restrict unauthorized access.

While we strive for security, no system is completely secure. Please use the app in a secure environment.

4. Data Retention and Deletion Policy

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements:

  • Personal data: Retained while your account is active.
  • Messaging data: Retained for up to 90 days for dispute resolution, after which it is permanently deleted.
  • Transaction data: Retained for five years to comply with legal and financial regulations.

To delete your account and data, contact us at birdlovershelp@gmail.com.

5. Your Rights and How to Exercise Them

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your data.
  • Objection: Opt-out of data processing for marketing purposes.
  • Control Over Contact Information: Users can update or remove their phone numbers from ads at any time through the app's settings or by editing the ad details.

Contact us to exercise your rights at birdlovershelp@gmail.com.
